`picaso` enables the: 1) computation of exoplanet and brown dwarf spectroscopy in transmission, emission or reflected light, 2) 1D climate modeling of brown dwarfs and exoplanets, 3) fitting spectroscopic data to models.

Major Features
--------------

- `Reflected Light Spectroscopy of planets <https://natashabatalha.github.io/picaso/notebooks/1_GetStarted.html>`_
- `Thermal Emission Spectroscopy of planets and brown dwarfs <https://natashabatalha.github.io/picaso/tutorials.html#basics-of-thermal-emission>`_
- `Transit Spectroscopy of planets <https://natashabatalha.github.io/picaso/notebooks/5_AddingTransitSpectrum.html>`_
- `Phase Curves of planets <https://natashabatalha.github.io/picaso/notebooks/9e_PhaseCurves.html>`_
- `1D Climate modeling of planets and brown dwarfs <https://natashabatalha.github.io/picaso/notebooks/climate/12a_BrownDwarf.html>`_
- `Fitting models to data <https://natashabatalha.github.io/picaso/notebooks/fitdata/GridSearch.html>`_
